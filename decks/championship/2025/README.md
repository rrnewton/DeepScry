## Notes on Accuracy

All four decklists sourced directly from the official **magic.gg** article "Magic World Championship 31 Top 8 Players and Decks," which was fetched in full and provides the authoritative card-by-card lists. High confidence across all four.

Seth Manfield won the finals 3–0 against Akira Shibata in an Izzet Lessons mirror match. Manfield went 9–0 in games on Top 8 Sunday, defeating Ken Yukuhiro (Sultai Reanimator), Derrick Davis (Izzet Lessons), then Shibata in the finals. This made Manfield the **third player in Magic history to win the World Championship twice** (joining Jon Finkel and Kai Budde as repeat champions).

The Top 8 consisted of 7 Izzet/Stormchaser Talent decks and 1 Sultai Reanimator (Ken Yukuhiro). Two Temur Otters builds also appeared (Henry and Huschenbeth).
