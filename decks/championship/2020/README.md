## Notes on Accuracy

All four decklists sourced from the Star City Games "World Championship XXVI Decklists Unveiled" article, which published the full 75-card lists. PVDDR and Carvalho further cross-checked against TappedOut/MTGArena Zone. Manfield's Mono Red confirmed from TappedOut. High confidence across all four.

PVDDR defeated Carvalho 4–2 (best-of-7) in the Grand Finals to claim his second World Championship title. The tournament was the first major Standard event after Theros Beyond Death release (February 2020). PVDDR was the lone Azorius Control pilot among the final Top 8 (versus Mono-Red, Temur Reclamation, and Jeskai Fires).

## Card Pool Context

Standard at MWCXXVI was **Throne of Eldraine + Theros Beyond Death** (just released). Fires of Invention and Teferi, Time Raveler were defining pillars; both would later be banned from Standard. PVDDR's Azorius Control hinged on Omen of the Sea, Shatter the Sky, and The Birth of Meletis from the newly-released Theros Beyond Death. Manfield's Mono Red used Embercleave (Throne of Eldraine) and Torbran.
