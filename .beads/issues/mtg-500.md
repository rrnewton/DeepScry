# Description

Test all behavioral aspects of Divine Offering in MTG Forge-rs.

Card: cardsfolder/d/divine_offering.txt
Set: LEG (mtg-386) [sideboard of rogue_rogerbrand mtg-387]
Test puzzle: test_puzzles/divine_offering_destroys_artifact.pzl

Card text:
  {1}{W} Instant
  Destroy target artifact. You gain life equal to its mana value.

Script:
  A:SP$ Destroy | ValidTgts$ Artifact | SubAbility$ DBGainLife
  SVar:DBGainLife:DB$ GainLife | Defined$ You | LifeAmount$ X
  SVar:X:Targeted$CardManaCost

Findings (2026-05-29_#2461(53f1d817), compat-oldschool-wave6):

1. [x] Parses as {1}{W} Instant.
2. [x] SP$ Destroy ValidTgts$ Artifact works: targets the opponent's
       artifact, destroys it, moves it to graveyard. Verified via game log.
3. [BROKEN] The chained DB$ GainLife with LifeAmount$ X where
   X = Targeted$CardManaCost does NOT grant life. Casting it on a mana-value-3
   artifact (Jalum Tome) leaves the caster at 20 life (should be 23).
   - Same class of dynamic-GainLife gap as Swords to Plowshares (mtg-297):
     a GainLife whose amount is computed from a target/board property
     (here Targeted$CardManaCost; for Swords it is target power) is not
     evaluated; the life gain is silently skipped.
   - Card is functional but strictly weaker than printed (no life gain).

Reproducer:

```sh
./target/release/mtg tui \
  --start-state test_puzzles/divine_offering_destroys_artifact.pzl \
  --p1=fixed --p2=zero \
  --p1-fixed-inputs="cast Divine Offering;*;*" --p2-fixed-inputs="" \
  --stop-on-choice=6 --seed 42 --verbosity 3
```

Observed: "Divine Offering (3) destroys Jalum Tome (10)" but P1 "Life: 20"
(no gain; expected 23).

Blocked on: mtg-297 (dynamic GainLife amount from target property).

CARD STATUS: PARTIAL — Destroy half works; "gain life equal to mana value" silently dropped (dynamic GainLife, blocked on mtg-297).
