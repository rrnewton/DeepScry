- Decks: decks/fuzz_white_aggro.dck vs decks/fuzz_black_control.dck

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Run: ./target/release/mtg tui --seed 1 --p1 heuristic --p2 heuristic -v 2 decks/fuzz_white_aggro.dck decks/fuzz_black_control.dck
2. Royal Assassin activates {T} ability, becoming tapped
