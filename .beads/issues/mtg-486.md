Card file: cardsfolder/b/black_vise.txt
Card text:
  {1} Artifact
  As Black Vise enters, choose an opponent.
  At the beginning of the chosen player's upkeep, Black Vise deals X damage
  to that player, where X is the number of cards in their hand minus 4.

Findings (2026-05-30_#2530(f7a005ca), compat-wave14-jeskai):

1. [x] Parses as {1} Artifact.
2. [x] Upkeep trigger now FIRES (after the wave14 phase-trigger
   DealDamageToTriggeredPlayer fix). Routes Defined\$ ChosenPlayer to the
   active player and evaluates the count against them.
3. [BROKEN] Deals 0 damage. Two missing engine pieces (filed as mtg-cuf0e):
   - Count\$ValidHand <selector>/Minus.4 — CountExpression has no
     hand-zone counting nor /Minus.N arithmetic modifier => parses to
     Fixed(0). So X is always 0.
   - K:ETBReplacement ChoosePlayer + ValidPlayer\$ Player.Chosen — no chosen
     player is recorded; in 2-player it is always the lone opponent, but the
     gate/Defined\$ ChosenPlayer need a stored chosen PlayerId.
   The wave14 loader change ALREADY emits the right effect shape; once
   mtg-cuf0e lands Black Vise should work with no further loader edits.
