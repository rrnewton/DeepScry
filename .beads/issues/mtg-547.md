# Description

Test all behavioral aspects of Swords to Plowshares in MTG Forge-rs.

Card: cardsfolder/s/swords_to_plowshares.txt
Set: LEA
Deck: rogue_rogerbrand (mtg-387)

Card text:
  Swords to Plowshares {W}, Instant
  Exile target creature. Its controller gains life equal to its power.
  A:SP$ ChangeZone | ValidTgts$ Creature | Origin$ Battlefield | Destination$ Exile | SubAbility$ DBGainLife
  SVar:DBGainLife:DB$ GainLife | Defined$ TargetedController | LifeAmount$ X
  SVar:X:Targeted$CardPower

Findings (2026-05-29_#2430(048328c1), compat-oldschool-wave1):

1. [x] Casts from hand at instant speed; targets a creature.
2. [x] Exile works — target creature is moved to exile.
   Evidence: "Swords to Plowshares (3) exiles Shivan Dragon (11)".
3. [BROKEN] Life gain does NOT happen. With STP exiling an opponent's
   Shivan Dragon (power 5), the controller's life stays at 20 (should be
   25). The DBGainLife SubAbility with LifeAmount$ X where X = Targeted$
   CardPower is not resolving — likely because the targeted creature has
   already left the battlefield (exiled by the parent ChangeZone) when the
   SubAbility tries to read its power, so X resolves to 0 (needs LKI).
   This is the EXISTING bug mtg-297 (Swords to Plowshares: exiles but does
   not grant life). Not fixed in this wave — deferred.

Reproducer:

```sh
cat > /tmp/stp.pzl <<'P'
[metadata]

turn=2

./target/release/mtg tui --start-state /tmp/stp.pzl --p1=heuristic --p2=zero \
  --stop-on-choice=8 --json --seed 42 --verbosity 3
```

Expected: "Player 2 gains 5 life" (Shivan Dragon power 5). Actual: no life change.

CARD STATUS: PARTIAL — exile works; controller life gain broken (see mtg-297).
