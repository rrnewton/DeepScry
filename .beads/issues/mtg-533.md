BUG: On a cross-player cast (P2 casts Psionic Blast targeting P1), the "2 damage to you"
half lands on the TARGET's controller (P1) instead of the CASTER (P2). User log:
  Psionic Blast (109) deals 4 damage to P1 (life: 12)
  Psionic Blast (109) deals 2 damage to P1 (life: 12)   <-- should be 2 to P2 (the caster)
Both halves hit P1; the caster took no self-damage. Expected: 4 to the chosen target (P1)
AND 2 to the caster (P2).

LIKELY REGRESSION VECTOR: the live card script now carries `DamageMap$ True` on the primary
SP$ DealDamage which the script tested at original closure (Findings 2026-05-29) did NOT have.
Current script:

  SVar:DBDealDamage:DB$ DealDamage | Defined$ You | NumDmg$ 2 | SubAbility$ DBDamageResolve
The `Defined$ You` self-damage sub-ability appears to resolve its recipient to the wrong player
under DamageMap aggregation / cross-player context.

half in a cross-player cast (opponent casts at you). See coordinator memory
