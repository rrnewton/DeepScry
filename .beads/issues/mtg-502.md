Findings (2026-05-30_#2488(f9fcef95), compat-oldschool-wave11):

1. [x] Parses as {14}{G} 10/10 Creature Elemental Dragon.
2. [x] K:Flying and K:Trample register (unit test asserts both keywords on
   the instantiated card).
3. [x] Castable from hand for its mana cost; resolves to a 10/10 on the
   battlefield. Verified via game log (cast Earthquake Dragon -> enters as
   10/10).
4. [x] Flying honored in combat: a ground blocker (Grizzly Bears) cannot
   block it; 10 combat damage hits the defending player. Verified e2e.
5. [BROKEN] Graveyard-return activated ability never offered:
   A:AB$ ChangeZone | Cost$ 2 G Sac<1/Land> | Origin$ Graveyard
        | Destination$ Hand | ActivationZone$ Graveyard
   With Earthquake Dragon in the graveyard and lands available, the
   {2}{G},Sac-a-land: return-to-hand ability is NOT enumerated as an
   available action (only 'pass' and 'play Forest' shown). Root cause:
   ActivatedAbility has no activation_zone field, so all activated
   abilities are implicitly battlefield-only and graveyard activations are
   never surfaced.
   Filed as: mtg-d8zuh (Bug: ActivationZone$ Graveyard activated abilities
   never offered) — generalizes to unearth / graveyard-recursion engines.
6. [unverified] ReduceCost static (cost {X} less where X = total mana value
   of Dragons you control). Not exercised; deferred.

## FIXED (2026-05-31_#2558(7af638d8), compat-finish-rogue)

fix(mtg-d8zuh): ActivationZone$ Graveyard — add activation_zone field to
ActivatedAbility, parse ActivationZone$ in card.rs, enumerate graveyard
cards in push_activatable_abilities. AB$ ChangeZone | Origin$ Graveyard |
Destination$ Hand now maps to MoveSelfBetweenZones (new case in
effect_converter.rs). priority.rs patches the self_target() placeholder to
the real card_id. HeuristicController now classifies MoveSelfBetweenZones as
ZoneReturn and activates it during main phase.
