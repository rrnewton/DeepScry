# Description

Test all behavioral aspects of Psychic Purge in MTG Forge-rs (1994 Old School, mtg-561).

Card file: cardsfolder/p/psychic_purge.txt
Card text:
  {U} Sorcery
  Psychic Purge deals 1 damage to any target.
  When a spell or ability an opponent controls causes you to discard Psychic
  Purge, that player loses 5 life.

Script:
  A:SP\$ DealDamage | ValidTgts\$ Any | NumDmg\$ 1
  T:Mode\$ Discarded | ValidCard\$ Card.Self | ValidCause\$ SpellAbility.OppCtrl | Execute\$ TrigLoseLife
  SVar:TrigLoseLife:DB\$ LoseLife | Defined\$ TriggeredCauseController | LifeAmount\$ 5

Findings (2026-05-30_#2530(f7a005ca), compat-wave14-jeskai):

1. [x] Parses as {U} Sorcery.
2. [x] PRIMARY mode: SP\$ DealDamage 1 to any target WORKS.
   Reproducer (P0 has Psychic Purge + 3 Islands, P1 no creatures):

```sh
cat > /tmp/pp.pzl <<P
[metadata]
Name:Psychic Purge deals 1
[state]
turn=2
activeplayer=p0
activephase=MAIN1
p0life=20
p0hand=Psychic Purge
p0library=Island; Island; Island; Island; Island
p0battlefield=Island; Island; Island
p1life=20
p1library=Plains; Plains; Plains; Plains; Plains
P
./target/release/mtg tui --start-state /tmp/pp.pzl --p1=fixed --p2=zero --p1-fixed-inputs="cast Psychic Purge;pass" --stop-on-choice=6 --json --seed 42 --verbosity 3
```
   Expected: `Psychic Purge (3) deals 1 damage to Player 2 (life: 19)`
3. [BROKEN] Secondary trigger silently dropped: there is no
   TriggerEvent::Discarded, so `T:Mode\$ Discarded` is ignored at load.
   The "opponent loses 5 life when they make you discard this" punisher does
   nothing. Engine gap filed as mtg-648.

CARD STATUS: PARTIAL — 1-damage primary mode works; the Discarded
opponent-punisher trigger is unsupported (mtg-648).
