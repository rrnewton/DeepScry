# Description

Card Compatibility: Psychic Purge (cardsfolder/p/psychic_purge.txt) — 1994 Old
School (Jeskai Aggro, mtg-561).

STATUS: WORKING (2026-06-09_#3106(b3281a33), claude/compat-psychic-purge).

Card:
  {U} Sorcery
  Psychic Purge deals 1 damage to any target.
  When a spell or ability an opponent controls causes you to discard Psychic
  Purge, that player loses 5 life.

Aspects:
1. [WORKING] Parses as {U} Sorcery.
2. [WORKING] PRIMARY: SP$ DealDamage 1 to any target.
   Evidence: "Psychic Purge (3) deals 1 damage to Player 2 (life: 19)".
3. [WORKING] (newly implemented, mtg-648) Secondary punisher: when an OPPONENT's
   spell/ability causes you to discard Psychic Purge, that player loses 5 life.
   Implemented via the new TriggerEvent::Discarded self-trigger fired on the
   discarded card's LKI (CR 603.6/603.10), gated by requires_opponent_cause
   (ValidCause$ SpellAbility.OppCtrl), with LoseLife 5 targeting the discard's
   CAUSE controller (threaded explicitly as discard_card's `cause` param).
   Evidence (Mind Rot, opponent's spell, forces the discard):
     "Player 2 discards Psychic Purge"
     "Player 1 loses 5 life (life: 15)"   <- Mind Rot's caster
   Self-discard (own cleanup) correctly does NOT punish (both stay at 20).

KNOWN MINOR GAP (mtg-894, does not block WORKING): the punisher does not yet
fire when an opponent's TRIGGERED ABILITY (Hypnotic Specter's random discard)
is what discards Psychic Purge — that discard path resolves through the shared
execute_effect with cause=None. The discard-SPELL vector (the realistic one in
this deck) is fully covered. Stricter-than-printed, never wrong-direction.

Tests:
  - Unit: game::actions::tests::effects::test_card_compat_psychic_purge.
  - E2E: tests/psychic_purge_discard_punisher_e2e.sh.

== UPDATE (2026-06-09, claude/compat-psychic-purge) ==
The previously-noted gap (opponent TRIGGERED-ability forced discards, e.g.
Hypnotic Specter) is now ALSO covered (mtg-894 CLOSED): the discard logic was
extracted into execute_discard_effect(effect, cause) and check_triggers_inner
routes trigger-forced discards with cause = the trigger controller. Psychic
Purge now punishes opponent discard SPELLS (Mind Rot/Hymn/Mind Twist) AND
opponent triggered-ability discards (Hypnotic Specter). Covered by
tests/psychic_purge_discard_punisher_e2e.sh +
tests/psychic_purge_triggered_discard_punisher_e2e.sh.
