== Findings (2026-05-29_#2462(132ce6cc)) ==

1. [x] Parses as a Land with no mana cost.
2. [x] All three activated abilities are parsed by the loader — verified by
   parser-shape regression `test_card_compat_mishras_factory` (mana
   ability is_mana_ability=true, animate produces
   Effect::SetBasePowerToughness with power=2/toughness=2, pump produces
   Effect::PumpCreature with power_bonus=1/toughness_bonus=1).
3. [x] Mana ability {T}: Add {C} produces colorless mana — verified by
   `test_load_mishras_factory_colorless_mana` (mtg-engine/tests/card_
   loading.rs) and exercised live by deck-vs-deck heuristic games.
4. [x] Animate ability {1}: becomes a 2/2 Assembly-Worker artifact creature
   until end of turn works end-to-end — verified by `test_mishras_
   factory_animates_and_is_eligible_attacker` (mtg-engine/tests/
   puzzle_e2e.rs): becomes Land + Artifact + Creature with subtype
   Assembly-Worker, current P/T 2/2, eligible attacker; reverts to a
   non-creature non-artifact Land at end-of-turn cleanup.
   Gameplay log evidence (factory_pump.pzl puzzle, p1=fixed, seed 42):
     'Mishra's Factory activates ability: Mishra's Factory becomes a 2/2
       Assembly-Worker artifact creature until end of turn. It's still a
       land.'
     'Mishra's Factory base P/T set to 2/2'
     'Mishra's Factory becomes Artifact + Creature'
5. [PARTIAL] Pump ability {T}: target Assembly-Worker gets +1/+1 — PARSER
   layer works (test asserts the Effect::PumpCreature is present), but
   the activation-time menu does NOT surface this third ability in real
   games. With two Factories on the battlefield and one animated to
   Assembly-Worker (so the other has a valid target), the action menu
   only ever offers ONE activatable ability per Factory:

     [Your_Main1] Player 1 available actions:
       [0] pass
       [1] activate Mishra's Factory   <-- Factory A (animate)
       [2] activate Mishra's Factory   <-- Factory B (animate)
       (NO third entry for Pump targeting Factory A's Assembly-Worker)

   Probable root cause: when the engine enumerates activated abilities
   for a card with two A: lines that share the same Tap cost (one mana,
   one pump), the listing logic in mtg-engine/src/game/game_loop/
   actions.rs picks the FIRST tap-cost ability and de-dups the rest.
   Since the mana ability is rendered as a separate "tap-for-mana"
   entry rather than an "activate" entry, the pump ability is collapsed
   under the same "activate Mishra's Factory" menu slot as Animate (and
   only the first of those two surfaces). Needs deeper investigation
   in get_available_actions / list_activated_abilities path; the engine
   should offer ALL non-mana activated abilities whose cost is currently
   payable.

   File this as a separate Bug: issue (engine gap, not card-specific).

6. [WORKING] No-cost summoning sickness (Factory is a land — see CR
   302.1; backdated turn_entered_battlefield=0 in the puzzle test
   verifies that an animated Factory with no SS is eligible to attack
   in the same turn it animates).
7. [WORKING] End-of-turn cleanup: animated Factory reverts to
   Land-only (not Artifact, not Creature, no Assembly-Worker subtype)
   via cleanup_temporary_effects() — verified by the puzzle e2e test.
