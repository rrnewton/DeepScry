# Description

Card Compatibility: Paralyze (cardsfolder/p/paralyze.txt) — 1994 Old School
(Mono Black Rogerbrand, mtg-560).

STATUS: WORKING (2026-06-09_#3084(f6c4713f), claude/compat-1994-wave3).

All three printed abilities verified:
1. [WORKING] {B} Aura — Enchant creature. Casts, targets a creature, attaches.
   Evidence: "Player 1 casts Paralyze ... -> targeting Grizzly Bears ...
   Paralyze enchants Grizzly Bears".
2. [WORKING] ETB: tap enchanted creature (T:ChangesZone Execute$ TrigTap). The
   creature is tapped on resolution.
3. [WORKING] Doesn't untap during its controller's untap step
   (R:Event$ Untap | Layer$ CantHappen -> GrantKeyword(DoesNotUntap)). On the
   controller's untap steps the creature stays "locked tapped".
4. [WORKING] (newly implemented, mtg-646) "At the beginning of the upkeep of
   enchanted creature's controller, that player may pay {4}; if they do, untap
   the creature." Fires on the HOST controller's upkeep (not the Aura
   controller's) via the new enchanted_controller_turn_only trigger flag, and
   untaps the enchanted creature ONLY when {4} is paid (shared UnlessCost
   machinery, switched). See mtg-646 for the engine work.

Tests:
- Unit: game/actions/tests/effects.rs::test_card_compat_paralyze (parser shape:
  DoesNotUntap static + the pay-{4} UnlessCostWrapper{UntapPermanent} upkeep
  trigger).
- E2E: tests/paralyze_pay4_untap_e2e.sh (pay vs can't-pay, seed 42).

KNOWN MINOR SUB-ISSUE (does not block WORKING; tracked here as follow-up):
On the enchanted creature's FIRST untap step immediately after Paralyze's
ETB-tap, the doesn't-untap lock does not yet apply, so the creature untaps once
before the lock engages on subsequent turns. This is in the
R:Event$ Untap | Layer$ CantHappen replacement lowering, independent of the
pay-{4} trigger (mtg-646), and is identical on clean integration. Lower
priority cosmetic-strictness gap; the card is otherwise fully playable.
