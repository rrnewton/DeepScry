== Findings (2026-05-30_#2525(d40c4206), compat-wave12-monoblack) — PARTIAL ==

Paralyze (cardsfolder/p/paralyze.txt) — Enchant creature Aura, {B}.
  K:Enchant:Creature
  T:... ChangesZone ... Execute$ TrigTap         (ETB: tap enchanted)
  R:Event$ Untap | Layer$ CantHappen | ValidCard$ Creature.EnchantedBy
                                                  (doesn't untap)
  T:Mode$ Phase | Phase$ Upkeep | ValidPlayer$ Player.EnchantedController
    | Execute$ TrigUntap                          (may pay {4} to untap)

1. [WORKING] Parses as Enchantment - Aura, {B}, with Keyword Enchant:Creature.
2. [WORKING] ETB tap: 'When Paralyze enters, tap enchanted creature.' The
   ChangesZone trigger fires and the enchanted creature is tapped on entry.
3. [WORKING] "Enchanted creature doesn't untap during its controller's untap
   step." NOW FIXED. The R:Event$ Untap | Layer$ CantHappen replacement is
   lowered (mtg-engine/src/loader/card.rs parse_static_abilities) into a
   continuous StaticAbility::GrantKeyword{ DoesNotUntap, CreatureEnchantedBy }.
   The untap step (mtg-engine/src/game/game_loop/steps.rs) skips any permanent
   whose has_keyword_with_effects(.., DoesNotUntap) is true. New keyword
   Keyword::DoesNotUntap. Generalizes to ALL Event$ Untap | Layer$ CantHappen
   locks (Exhaustion, Sleep, etc.).
4. [BROKEN] "At the beginning of the upkeep of enchanted creature's controller,
   that player may pay {4}. If they do, untap the creature." The phase-trigger
   parser only handles ValidPlayer$ You; it drops ValidPlayer$
   Player.EnchantedController, so the optional pay-{4}-to-untap trigger never
   fires. SAFE failure mode (the lock just holds — STRICTER than printed, not
   exploitable). Filed as mtg-646. A naive fix that untaps unconditionally
   would make Paralyze free to escape — the UnlessCost$ 4 gate MUST be honored.

not yet rules-complete. Issue stays OPEN pending mtg-646.
