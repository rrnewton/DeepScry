== Engine work (this branch) ==
New `Defined$ Enchanted` infrastructure (shared, reusable):
- core/entity.rs: ENCHANTED_TARGET_ID sentinel + is_enchanted_target()/enchanted_target().
- triggers.rs: TriggerContext.enchanted (the Aura's Card::attached_to) + with_enchanted();
  resolve_effect_placeholder resolves Tap/Untap{enchanted_target} to ctx.enchanted.
- actions/mod.rs: thread attached_to into the trigger context; TapPermanent now
  emits a "<name> becomes tapped" gamelog and guards the enchanted sentinel.
- effect_converter.rs: DB$ Tap/Untap | Defined$ Enchanted -> enchanted_target sentinel.
This infra also unblocks the Untap half of the pay-{4} escape (mtg-646) and any
other Aura `Defined$ Enchanted` Tap/Untap.
