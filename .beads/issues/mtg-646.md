# Description

Paralyze's third ability:
  T:Mode$ Phase | Phase$ Upkeep | ValidPlayer$ Player.EnchantedController
    | TriggerZones$ Battlefield | Execute$ TrigUntap
  SVar:TrigUntap:DB$ Untap | Defined$ Enchanted | UnlessCost$ 4
    | UnlessPayer$ EnchantedController | UnlessSwitched$ True
"At the beginning of the upkeep of enchanted creature's controller, that
player MAY pay {4}. If the player does, untap the creature."

The phase-trigger parser (mtg-engine/src/loader/card.rs, Mode$ Phase branch)
only recognizes ValidPlayer$ You (controller-only). It does NOT handle
ValidPlayer$ Player.EnchantedController, so this optional untap trigger never
fires. That is currently the SAFE failure mode: the enchanted creature stays
locked (the doesn't-untap lock from mtg-529 holds), which is STRICTER than
printed (the controller cannot pay {4} to escape).

Wiring it requires THREE pieces that don't yet exist together:
  1. ValidPlayer$ Player.EnchantedController support in the phase-trigger
     parser (fire on the ENCHANTED creature's controller's upkeep — a
     different player than the Aura's controller).
  2. Defined$ Enchanted resolution for the Untap target.
  3. The UnlessCost$ 4 | UnlessPayer$ EnchantedController | UnlessSwitched$ True
     optional-payment wrapper applied to Effect::UntapPermanent. UnlessCost
     infra exists (effect_converter.rs parse_unless_cost) but is not threaded
     through this hand-rolled phase-trigger SVar path.

DANGER: a naive fix that fires the trigger and untaps unconditionally (ignoring
UnlessCost) would make Paralyze FREE to escape every upkeep — strictly worse
than not firing. The UnlessCost gate MUST be honored.

Blocks Card Compatibility: Paralyze (mtg-529) reaching WORKING.

== UPDATE 2026-06-09 (claude/compat-1994-enchant-triggers-v2) ==
Piece 2 of 3 (Defined$ Enchanted resolution for the Untap target) is now DONE:
`DB$ Untap | Defined$ Enchanted` lowers to the enchanted_target sentinel and
resolve_effect_placeholder resolves it to the Aura's attached_to (shared infra
added for Paralyze's ETB-tap, mtg-529). STILL REMAINING:
1. Mode$ Phase trigger parser support for ValidPlayer$ Player.EnchantedController
   (fire on the ENCHANTED creature's controller's upkeep — a different player
   than the Aura's controller).
3. The UnlessCost$ 4 | UnlessPayer$ EnchantedController | UnlessSwitched$ True
   optional-payment wrapper threaded onto the Untap in the phase-trigger SVar
   path. (UnlessCost infra exists in effect_converter; not yet applied here.)
The DANGER note still holds: the UnlessCost gate MUST be honored — an
unconditional untap would make Paralyze free to escape every upkeep.
