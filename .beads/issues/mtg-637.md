# Description

Follow-up to mtg-490 (source-filtered damage prevention / Circle of Protection). The Effect::PreventDamageFromSource chooser (get_valid_targets_for_ability in mtg-engine/src/game/actions/targeting.rs) offers matching-colour CREATURES on the battlefield + matching-colour SPELLS on the stack, excluding the Circle's own enchantment. GAP: a matching-colour NON-creature permanent that can deal damage (e.g. a coloured damage artifact/enchantment) is not offered as a chooseable source, though CR 615 would allow it. We restrict to creatures+spells for AI auto-pick quality. Fix: offer any matching-colour permanent with a damage-dealing ability, still excluding the CoP itself and ranking threats first. Low priority — no card in the 6 old-school playtest decks exercises this.
