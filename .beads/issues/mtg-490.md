# Description

Card Compatibility: Circle of Protection: Red (cardsfolder/c/circle_of_protection_red.txt).
In 4 of the 6 1994 Old-School playtest decks: rogue (mtg-387), thedeck (mtg-413), robots (mtg-559), jeskai (mtg-561). Full per-aspect skeleton checklist preserved in git history at HEAD:.beads/issues/mtg-490.md.

== RESOLUTION (2026-05-30, branch compat-damage-prevention-cop) — WORKING, CLOSED ==

Implemented a GENERAL source-filtered damage-prevention replacement-effect
construct (CR 615.1, 615.6) and wired Circle of Protection: Red end-to-end.

General construct (NOT card-special-cased):
- `mtg-engine/src/core/prevention.rs`: strong-typed `DamageSourceFilter`
  (Color / SpecificSource / ColoredSource), `PreventionScope`
  (AllThisTurn / NextEvent / NextPoints), and `DamagePreventionShield`.
- `Player::source_prevention_shields: Vec<DamagePreventionShield>` holds the
  continuous until-end-of-turn shields (cleared in the cleanup step,
  CR 514.2, alongside the existing blanket `damage_prevention`).
- `Effect::PreventDamageFromSource { protected, color, source }`
  (core/effects.rs) is the resolved effect.
- Parsing: `effect_converter.rs` `ApiType::ChooseSource` arm reads the source
  colour from the tokenized `Choices$ Card.<Color>Source` filter (helper
  `choose_source_color`, NO substring matching), generalizing across all five
  Circles of Protection. Non-CoP `ChooseSource` shapes (Martyr's Cause
  `Card,Emblem`) decline conversion.
- Damage-path hook: `apply_source_prevention_shields` consulted in BOTH combat
  attribution (`actions/combat.rs`, per attacker, before aggregation so logs
  stay accurate) and direct/burn damage (`deal_damage`) via the transient
  `GameState::current_damage_source` (#[serde(skip)], set/cleared around
  effect resolution in `resolve_spell_execute_effects`). Prevents the chosen
  source's next damage event entirely, then the shield expires.
- Targeting: `get_valid_targets_for_ability` offers matching-colour creatures
  (battlefield) + spells (stack), excluding the Circle's own enchantment.
- Heuristic AI activates CoP during combat (classified as PreventDamage).

Evidence (mtg tui reproducers):
- PREVENTED (red combat): test_puzzles/circle_of_protection_red_prevents.pzl —
  "Prevented 2 damage to Player 2 from Ironclaw Orcs (5)", life stays 20.
- PREVENTED (red burn): CoP:Red chosen on a Lightning Bolt on the stack →
  "Prevented 3 damage to Player 2 from Lightning Bolt", life stays 20.
- NOT PREVENTED (green): test_puzzles/circle_of_protection_red_nonred.pzl —
  "Grizzly Bears deals 2 damage to Player 2 (life: 18)".
- CoP:White verified preventing a white attacker via the same construct.
- Heuristic-vs-heuristic prevents autonomously; deterministic across runs.

Tests:
- Parser-shape unit: test_card_compat_circle_of_protection_red,
  test_convert_choose_source_all_circle_colors,
  test_convert_choose_source_non_cop_returns_none (effect_converter.rs).
- Construct units: core::prevention::tests (3).
- Engine unit: test_source_prevention_shield_matches_only_chosen_red_source
  (actions/tests/effects.rs).
- E2E: tests/circle_of_protection_red_e2e.sh (positive + negative cases),
  auto-discovered by shell_script_tests.rs.

Determinism / info-independence: prevention uses only public card
characteristics (colours, chosen source identity); current_damage_source is
never serialized; same gamelog across repeated seeds and across server/client.

All five Circles of Protection (White/Blue/Black/Green) now work via the
identical colour-filter path (bonus). Follow-up (minor heuristic quality): a
coloured non-creature damaging permanent is not offered as a chosen source.
