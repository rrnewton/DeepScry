# Description

Card Compatibility: Falling Star

Card file: cardsfolder/f/falling_star.txt
Deck: 06_troll_disk_daniellebrunazzo (mtg-562, sideboard)

Card text:
  {2}{R}  Sorcery
  Flip Falling Star onto the playing area from a height of at least one foot.
  Falling Star deals 3 damage to each creature it lands on. Tap all creatures
  dealt damage by Falling Star. If Falling Star doesn't turn completely over at
  least once during the flip, it has no effect.

Script:
  A:SP$ FlipOntoBattlefield | SubAbility$ DBDamageTouched | AILogic$ DamageCreatures
  SVar:DBDamageTouched:DB$ DamageAll | ValidCards$ Creature.IsRemembered | NumDmg$ 3 | SubAbility$ DBTapAllDamaged
  SVar:DBTapAllDamaged:DB$ TapAll | ValidCards$ Creature.IsRemembered+DamagedBy | SubAbility$ DBCleanup
  SVar:DBCleanup:DB$ Cleanup | ClearRemembered$ True

Findings (2026-05-29_#2470(be2f61b4), wave10):

1. [x] Parses and is castable as {2}{R} Sorcery.
2. [BROKEN] On resolution Falling Star wrongly DESTROYS a land:
   "Falling Star (3) destroys Mountain (4)". The FlipOntoBattlefield handler
   appears to reuse the Chaos-Orb-style destroy-touched path instead of the
   damage path. Falling Star must NOT destroy anything.
3. [BROKEN] No creature is actually damaged: ValidCards$ Creature.IsRemembered
   matches the set of creatures the card physically "landed on", which the
   engine cannot simulate (dexterity card). The remembered set is empty, so
   the DamageAll/TapAll passes hit zero creatures.

Root cause: Falling Star is a dexterity ("flip onto the play area") card. Its
real targets are determined by physical landing position (Creature.IsRemembered
populated by FlipOntoBattlefield). The engine has no faithful simulation, and
the current FlipOntoBattlefield handling both (a) mis-fires a destroy and (b)
leaves the remembered set empty so the intended 3-damage-to-each-landed-creature
never happens.

Observed (WRONG): "Falling Star (3) destroys Mountain (4)"; no creature damaged.
Expected: no land destroyed; (in a faithful sim) 3 damage to each creature
landed on, then tap those creatures.

NEXT STEP: decide a deterministic model for the FlipOntoBattlefield damage
variant (e.g. "deals 3 to a random/AI-chosen creature" or "to all creatures")
that does NOT destroy lands, and route SP$ FlipOntoBattlefield + DamageAll
distinctly from Chaos Orb's destroy variant. Tracked here for a future pass.

CARD STATUS: BROKEN — dexterity flip card; wrongly destroys a land and damages
no creatures. Needs a deterministic FlipOntoBattlefield damage model.


Status (2026-05-31_#2549(221c7867), compat-finish-trolldisk) — HARD BLOCKER, left OPEN:

Falling Star is a DEXTERITY card ("Flip Falling Star onto the playing area from
a height of at least one foot ... deals 3 to each creature it lands on"). Its
real targets are determined by physical landing position, which a digital
engine cannot faithfully simulate. The current engine also mis-routes
SP$ FlipOntoBattlefield + DBDamageTouched through Chaos Orb's destroy path
(wrongly destroys a land) and leaves Creature.IsRemembered empty (no creature
damaged). Implementing a faithful version requires a DESIGN DECISION on a
deterministic, replay-safe, native==WASM damage model (random/AI-chosen
creatures vs all creatures) plus routing the DamageAll/TapAll branch distinctly
from the destroy branch.

Out of scope for this card-compat pass (needs an engine design decision, not a
card script tweak). Focused follow-up filed: mtg-665
(SP$ FlipOntoBattlefield + DBDamageTouched deterministic damage model).
Related: mtg-392 (Chaos Orb FlipOntoBattlefield self-target).

CARD STATUS: BROKEN — dexterity flip card; left OPEN pending mtg-665. This is
a documented hard blocker (sideboard 1-of in mtg-562).
