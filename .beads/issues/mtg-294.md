# Description

## Context
- Date: 2026-04-03
- Decks: decks/fuzz_white_aggro.dck vs decks/fuzz_black_control.dck
- Mode: heuristic-vs-heuristic, Seeds: 1, 5, 6, 10

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Run: ./target/release/mtg tui --seed 10 --p1 heuristic --p2 heuristic -v 2 decks/fuzz_white_aggro.dck decks/fuzz_black_control.dck
2. Observe Terror (black instant) targeting and destroying White Knight
3. Also observe Royal Assassin (black creature) targeting White Knight with its ability

## Expected Behavior
White Knight has 'Protection from black' (K:Protection from black in card definition).
Per CR 702.16, a permanent with protection from black:
- Can't be targeted by black spells
- Can't be targeted by abilities from black sources
- Can't be blocked by black creatures
- All damage from black sources is prevented
- Can't be enchanted/equipped by black Auras/Equipment

Terror and Royal Assassin are both black sources and should not be able to target White Knight.

## Actual Behavior
Terror targets and destroys White Knight. Royal Assassin targets White Knight with its activated ability.
The engine does not check protection when validating targets.

## Evidence
- Terror destroys White Knight in seed 10: Terror (108) destroys White Knight (52)
- Royal Assassin targets White Knight in seeds 1, 5, 6
- Command: ./target/release/mtg tui --seed 10 --p1 heuristic --p2 heuristic -v 2 decks/fuzz_white_aggro.dck decks/fuzz_black_control.dck
