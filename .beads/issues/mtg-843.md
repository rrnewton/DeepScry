Findings (2026-06-10_#3175(c6dbd34f), compat-2025-survey slot05):

[PARTIAL] Level 3 damage bonus silently dropped. L1 (Whenever you cast a noncreature spell, discard->draw) and L2 (noncreature spells cost {1} less, S:Mode$ ReduceCost) parse and work. L3 "your noncombat damage to opponents +2" uses DB$ ReplaceEffect | VarName$ DamageAmount — there is NO damage-INCREASE replacement layer in the engine (ApiType::ReplaceEffect does not exist in the parser; no converter arm). The R: line is dropped; L3 does nothing. Card is functional but strictly weaker than printed at L3.
