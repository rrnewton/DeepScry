== Findings (2026-05-29_#2449(b5fd60b7), compat-oldschool-wave4) ==

1. [x] Parses as {4} Artifact.
2. [PARTIAL] A:AB$ Tap | Cost$ 1 T | ValidTgts$ Artifact,Creature,Land — the
   Tap effect itself works, and casting Icy from mana is fine, BUT activation
   auto-targeted the SOURCE (Icy Manipulator itself) rather than an opponent
   permanent, with both fixed and heuristic controllers. The ability resolved
   but tapped itself, so the AI can't use it usefully.
   Filed as: Bug: AB$ Tap auto-targets its own source (mtg-zssaf).

== Findings (2026-06-01_#2587(1faf5ae6), compat-monoblack-v2) ==

FIXED. Two changes in heuristic_controller.rs (branch compat-monoblack-v2):

1. **should_cast_spell**: Added an arm for utility artifacts with non-mana
   activated abilities. The heuristic now casts Icy Manipulator when the
   opponent has permanents that the Tap ability can affect. Without this,
   the AI treated Icy as an unrecognised artifact and held it in hand.

2. **choose_targets** (has_tap_effect): When a spell or activated ability has
   a TapPermanent effect, the heuristic now prefers opponent permanents. Before
   the fix, the default target logic was used and selected the source itself
   (the only card in hand with the activated ability, so Icy Manipulator
   appeared in ValidTgts$ and was selected). Now: opponent creature first,
   fallback to any opponent permanent, last resort any valid target.
   CR 602.1b: target choice is part of activation; no rules violation
   regardless of which legal target is selected.
