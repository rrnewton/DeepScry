# Description

Random deck tournament (2025-11-23_#894) revealed many unhandled `Affected$` selectors in static ability parsing. These cause cards with complex static abilities to silently fail.

## Examples Found in Tournament

**Enchantment-related:**
- `Creature.EnchantedBy` - Targets creature enchanted by this aura
- `Creature.EnchantedBy+EnchantedBy Aura.Other` - Compound: enchanted by this AND another aura

**Equipment-related:**
- `Card.EquippedBy` - Targets card equipped by this equipment

**Tribal/Type-specific:**
- `Goblin.YouCtrl` - Goblins you control
- `Zombie.YouCtrl`, `Zombie.YouCtrl+Other` - Zombies (excluding/including self)
- `Creature.Ninja+Other+YouCtrl,Creature.Rogue+Other+YouCtrl` - Multiple types (OR)
- `Creature.Artifact+Other+YouCtrl` - e.g., Master of Etherium's buff
- `Creature.ChosenType+YouCtrl` - Based on chosen type
- `Creature.Cat+YouCtrl`, `Creature.Legendary+Other+YouCtrl`
- `Creature.nonHuman+Other+YouCtrl` - Negation selector

**Compound selectors:**
- `Creature.Land+YouCtrl` - Creature lands
- `Creature.YouCtrl+enchanted,Creature.YouCtrl+equipped` - State-based (OR)
- `Creature.SharesColorWith Equipped` - Relationship-based
- `Creature.sharesCreatureTypeWith Equipped` - Relationship-based

**Zone selectors:**
- `Card.TopLibrary+YouCtrl` - Top of library
- `Card.TopLibrary+YouCtrl+withFlying,Card.TopLibrary+YouCtrl+hasKeywordFlash` - With properties
- `Creature.YouOwn` (with `AffectedZone$ Hand`) - Zone-crossing

**Other parsing failures:**
- `You` - Player as Affected target
- Variable power/toughness: `AddPower$ X`, `AddPower$ Z` (fails parsing)

## Impact

Cards with these selectors have their static abilities silently ignored, making them unplayable or significantly weakened. This affects hundreds of cards including:
- Tribal lords (Goblin Chieftain, Death Baron, etc.)
- Equipment with complex effects
- Auras with variable buffs
- Cards caring about board state

## Location

Affected$ selector parsing in: `mtg-engine/src/loader/ability_parser.rs`

## Note

The `+` symbol in selectors means AND (conjunction), not foil indication. Example:
- `Creature.Artifact+Other+YouCtrl` = "Artifact creatures AND other AND you control"

## Progress (2025-11-29_#966)

**Implemented in c947c29:**
- ✅ `Creature.TYPE+YouCtrl` - e.g., `Creature.Goblin+YouCtrl`
- ✅ `Creature.TYPE+Other+YouCtrl` - e.g., `Creature.Goblin+Other+YouCtrl`
- ✅ `TYPE.YouCtrl` - e.g., `Goblin.YouCtrl`
- ✅ `TYPE.Other+YouCtrl` - e.g., `Goblin.Other+YouCtrl`
- ✅ `Creature.EnchantedBy` - Auras that modify enchanted creature
- ✅ Comma-separated selectors with multiple types

This enables tribal lords like Goblin Chieftain, Death Baron, Elvish Archdruid.

## Progress (2025-11-30_#1016)

**Implemented:**
- ✅ `Creature.Artifact+YouCtrl` - Card type-based selectors (not just subtypes)
- ✅ `Creature.Artifact+Other+YouCtrl` - e.g., Master of Etherium's buff
- ✅ Added `CreatureCardTypeYouControl` and `CreatureCardTypeOtherYouControl` variants

This enables artifact lords like Master of Etherium.

## Progress (2025-12-01_#1061)

**Implemented in b4df037:**
- ✅ `Creature.Land+YouCtrl` - Land creatures you control (LandCreaturesYouControl)
- ✅ `Creature.nonTYPE+Other+YouCtrl` - Negation patterns (CreatureNonTypeOtherYouControl)

This enables:
- Mikaeus, the Unhallowed: "Other non-Human creatures you control get +1/+1"
- Embodiment of Fury: "Land creatures you control have trample"
- Jyoti, Moag Ancient: "Land creatures you control have vigilance"

## Progress (2025-12-01_#1064(e014e8a))

**Implemented in e014e8a:**
- ✅ `Card.Self+equipped` - Self-buff when equipped (SelfWhenEquipped)
- ✅ `Card.Self+enchanted` - Self-buff when enchanted (SelfWhenEnchanted)
- ✅ `Creature.YouCtrl+equipped` - Buff equipped creatures (EquippedCreaturesYouControl)
- ✅ `Creature.YouCtrl+enchanted` - Buff enchanted creatures (EnchantedCreaturesYouControl)

This enables:
- Leonin Lightbringer, Kitesail Apprentice, Auriok Glaivemaster (+1/+1 when equipped)
- Thran Golem, Flaring Flame-Kin (+2/+2 when enchanted)
- Kemba, Kha Enduring, Hexgold Hoverwings (buff all equipped creatures)

## Progress (2025-12-01_#1069)

**Implemented:**
- ✅ `Card.EquippedBy` - Now parsed identically to `Creature.EquippedBy`

This enables 71 cards with equipment bonuses like:
- Demonmail Hauberk (+4/+2)
- Diamond Pick-Axe (+1/+1)
- Arc Spitter (adds abilities)

Rationale: `Card.EquippedBy` and `Creature.EquippedBy` are semantically equivalent because per CR 301.5 and CR 702.6, Equipment can only be attached to creatures.

**Still TODO:**
- `Creature.ChosenType+YouCtrl` - Based on chosen type (requires state tracking)
- State-based OR patterns (`+enchanted,+equipped` in same selector)
- Zone selectors (`Card.TopLibrary`, etc.)
- Relationship-based patterns (`SharesColorWith`, `sharesCreatureTypeWith`)
