# Description

Test all behavioral aspects of Chain Lightning in MTG Forge-rs (1994 Old School; decks mtg-561, mtg-562).

Card file: cardsfolder/c/chain_lightning.txt
Card text:
  {R} Sorcery
  Chain Lightning deals 3 damage to any target. Then that player or that
  permanent's controller may pay {R}{R}. If the player does, they may copy
  this spell and may choose a new target for that copy.

Script:
  A:SP\$ DealDamage | ValidTgts\$ Any | NumDmg\$ 3 | SubAbility\$ DBCopy1
  SVar:DBCopy1:DB\$ CopySpellAbility | Defined\$ Parent | Controller\$ TargetedOrController | UnlessPayer\$ TargetedOrController | UnlessCost\$ R R | MayChooseTarget\$ True

Findings (2026-05-30_#2530(f7a005ca), compat-wave14-jeskai):

1. [x] Parses as {R} Sorcery.
2. [x] PRIMARY mode: deals 3 damage to any target. Reproducer:

```sh
cat > /tmp/cl.pzl <<P
[metadata]
Name:Chain Lightning 3
[state]
turn=2
activeplayer=p0
activephase=MAIN1
p0life=20
p0hand=Chain Lightning
p0library=Mountain; Mountain; Mountain; Mountain; Mountain
p0battlefield=Mountain; Mountain; Mountain
p1life=20
p1library=Plains; Plains; Plains; Plains; Plains
P
./target/release/mtg tui --start-state /tmp/cl.pzl --p1=fixed --p2=zero --p1-fixed-inputs="cast Chain Lightning;pass" --stop-on-choice=8 --json --seed 42 --verbosity 3
```
   Expected: `Chain Lightning (3) deals 3 damage to Player 2 (life: 14)`
3. [BROKEN] The optional "pay {R}{R} to copy and re-target" sub-ability
   (DB\$ CopySpellAbility | Defined\$ Parent) is NOT implemented — see the
   long-standing mtg-152. The engine logs an INFO that copy is not yet
   implemented and the spell does not copy. The misleading gamelog line
   "Chain Lightning copies spell (may choose new targets)" should be
   suppressed until copy lands (tracked under mtg-152).

CARD STATUS: PARTIAL — 3-damage primary mode works; the optional spell-copy
chain is unimplemented (mtg-152).
