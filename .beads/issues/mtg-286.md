# Description

## Context
- Date: 2026-04-03
- Puzzle: puzzles/test_double_strike_damage.pzl
- Seed: 1
- Mode: heuristic-vs-heuristic

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Run: `./target/release/mtg tui --start-state puzzles/test_double_strike_damage.pzl --p1=heuristic --p2=heuristic --seed=1 --verbosity=3`
2. Observe Turn 1 output

## Expected Behavior
The puzzle starts at activephase=DECLAREBLOCKERS with Adorned Pouncer (1/1 Double Strike) already marked as Attacking against an opponent at 2 life with no blockers.

Combat damage should be dealt on Turn 1:
- First Strike: Adorned Pouncer deals 1 → life: 1
- Normal: Adorned Pouncer deals 1 → life: 0
- Player 2 should die immediately on Turn 1.

## Actual Behavior
Turn 1 output:
```
--- Declare Blockers Step ---
--- Combat Damage Step ---
--- End of Combat Step ---
```

No damage is dealt. The game continues to Turn 2, 3. Only on Turn 3 when Adorned Pouncer attacks through the normal full combat sequence does double strike damage fire correctly (1 first strike + 1 normal = 2 total).

The puzzle is essentially a no-op on its first turn — the Attacking state is loaded but combat damage is not resolved.

## Rules Notes
- CR 510.1: Combat damage is assigned during the combat damage step
- CR 510.2: All combat damage is dealt simultaneously
- The puzzle specifically tests that mid-combat state is restored correctly
- The attacking creature's Attacking status appears to be lost or not recognized by the combat damage step when loaded from puzzle state

## Evidence
- Command: see above
- Full log shows Turn 1 has empty combat damage step, Turn 3 correctly deals 1+1=2 damage
