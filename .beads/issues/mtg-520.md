# Description

Card Compatibility: Maze of Ith (cardsfolder/m/maze_of_ith.txt)

Per .claude/skills/compatibility_tracking/SKILL.md.

== Card Status ==

CARD STATUS: PARTIAL — primary 'Untap target attacking creature' parses
and runs, but the SubAbility$ DBPump (DB$ Effect | ReplacementEffects$
RPrevent1,RPrevent2) that prevents combat damage on the untapped creature
is silently dropped by the loader. Net effect: Maze of Ith untaps the
attacker but the attacker still deals damage that turn — strictly weaker
than printed.

== Parser-shape test ==

mtg-engine/src/game/actions/tests/effects.rs::test_card_compat_maze_of_ith
verifies the primary Untap effect parses and that Maze produces no mana
ability (it must NOT be a generic colorless source). The test does NOT
assert the SubAbility chain is present — that would fail at HEAD; the
status is documented in the test rustdoc.

== Engine gap ==

The loader needs to expand 'AB$ Untap … SubAbility$ DBPump' →
'SVar:DBPump:DB$ Effect | ReplacementEffects$ RPrevent1,RPrevent2 |
RememberObjects$ Targeted | ExileOnMoved$ Battlefield' into an
Effect::CreateContinuousEffect (or similar) chained alongside the
Untap on the activated ability's effect list. Damage-prevention
infrastructure exists in mtg-engine/src/game/state/damage.rs.

Status: BROKEN/PARTIAL — open until SubAbility chain is parsed and
runtime 'no damage dealt' is verified by puzzle/e2e.
