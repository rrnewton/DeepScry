== Findings (2026-05-29_#2449(b5fd60b7), compat-oldschool-wave4) ==

1. [x] Parses as {1}{B}{B} 2/2 Creature — Specter with K:Flying.
2. [x] Flying registered on the instantiated Card (keywords set).
3. [WORKING] T:Mode$ DamageDone | ValidSource$ Card.Self | ValidTarget$ Opponent
   | Execute$ TrigDiscard (DB$ Discard | Defined$ TriggeredTarget | NumCards$ 1
   | Mode$ Random) — when Hypnotic Specter deals combat damage to an opponent,
   THAT PLAYER (the damaged opponent) discards a card at random.

   BUG FOUND + FIXED this commit: the effect converter had no match arm for
   'Defined$ TriggeredTarget' / 'Defined$ TriggeredPlayer', so the discard
   player fell through to PlayerId::placeholder(). resolve_effect_placeholder
   (trigger path) then mapped the placeholder to ctx.controller — the ATTACKER.
   Result before fix: the Hypnotic Specter CONTROLLER discarded from their own
   hand instead of the player they hit (observed in deck game seed 7:
   'AI-Heuristic1 discards Dark Ritual' where Heuristic1 owned the Specter).

   FIX: converter now emits PlayerId::target_opponent() for
   TriggeredTarget/TriggeredPlayer; resolve_effect_placeholder maps the
   target_opponent sentinel for DiscardCards to ctx.opponent (the damaged
   player; CR 116.2c 2-player approximation, same scheme Mind Twist uses
   for ValidTgts$ Player — mtg-564). CR 701.8 (Discard), CR 603 (triggers).
