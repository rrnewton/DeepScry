Card: {X}{1}{B} Sorcery. "Spend only black mana on X. Drain Life deals X damage
to any target. You gain life equal to the damage dealt, but not more life than
the player's life total / the planeswalker's loyalty / the creature's toughness
before the damage was dealt."

CARD STATUS: WORKING (2026-06-09, claude/compat-1994-damage). Previously BROKEN.

== Aspects ==
1. [WORKING] Parses {X}{1}{B} Sorcery. The StoreSVar cap-computing chain lowers
   to silent Effect::NoOp nodes; the chain yields DealDamageXPaid (X damage) and
   GainLifeDynamic{DamageDealtCappedByTarget}. Unit: test_card_compat_drain_life.
2. [WORKING] Deals X damage to the chosen "any target" (player/creature/
   planeswalker). X is spent as black mana.
3. [WORKING] Life gain = min(damage dealt, target's pre-damage life/loyalty/
   toughness) — the cap. Verified:
   - Player cap: X=6 at a 3-life player -> deals 6 (lethal), gain 3.
     e2e test_drain_life_caps_lifegain_at_player_life.
   - Creature cap: X=4 at a 2/2 -> creature dies, gain 2.
     e2e test_drain_life_caps_lifegain_at_creature_toughness.
   - Non-overkill: X=1 at a 20-life player -> deal 1, gain 1.
4. [WORKING] No more "unimplemented effect 'StoreSVar'" warning spam (StoreSVar
   is now a recognized silent NoOp).

life, captured pre-damage). The cap is modeled directly rather than via a runtime
SVar store. Determinism verified (network-vs-local 0 divergence; byte-identical
