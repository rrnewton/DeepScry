# Description

## Card Compatibility: Drain Life — BROKEN (not covered by dynamic-GainLife work)

Investigated 2026-05-30_#2489 (branch compat-dynamic-gainlife-mtg297) while
implementing the general dynamic-amount GainLife construct (mtg-297). Drain Life
does NOT cleanly generalize from that construct and remains BROKEN.

Why it is harder than Swords/Divine Offering:
- Drain Life's HEAD ability is `A:SP$ StoreSVar` (a Forge SVar-store pseudo-API),
  not a damage/gainlife API. `StoreSVar` produces no recognized Effect today, so
  the entire spell is currently a no-op (does not even deal its X damage).
- The life gain is `DB$ GainLife | LifeAmount$ DrainedLifeCard` where
  `DrainedLifeCard = SVar$ Y/LimitMax.Limit`, `Y = Count$TotalDamageDoneByThisTurn`,
  with a per-target CAP (Limit = target's toughness / loyalty / life total before
  damage). I.e. 'gain life equal to the damage actually dealt, capped'.

Missing infrastructure (each its own gap):
1. `SP$ StoreSVar` head-ability support + the `ConditionDefined/Present/Compare`
   chain that selects the Limit expression by target type.
2. `Count$TotalDamageDoneByThisTurn` count expression (damage-dealt tracking).
3. A `DamageDealt`-with-cap dynamic amount (the `DynamicAmount::DamageDealt`
   enum variant is reserved in core/effects.rs but NOT wired).

Recommended path: implement `StoreSVar` + damage-dealt counting first, then wire
`DynamicAmount::DamageDealt` (clamped by the stored Limit) into GainLifeDynamic.

EFFECT_SUPPORT.md has a dedicated BROKEN row for the damage-drain shape.
